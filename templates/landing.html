Powerful Features

AI Extraction

Advanced LLM-powered extraction from PDF and DOCX resumes. Automatically identifies skills, experience, education, and contact information.

Schema Validation

Strict JSON Schema validation ensures data quality. Anti-hallucination techniques verify contact details against extracted text.

Smart Classification

Automatic role classification and seniority detection. Get instant insights into candidate profiles.

Advanced Search

Search by name, role, skills, or location. Filter by confidence scores and export results to CSV.
